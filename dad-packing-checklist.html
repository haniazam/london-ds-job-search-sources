Simple suitcase formula

  • Pack for 7–10 days, not 30 days.
  • Do laundry weekly.
  • Carry-on: passport, documents, medicines, chargers, one outfit.
  • Rain jacket + light sweater beats bulky outerwear.

Night-before check

  • Phone and power bank charged.
  • Passport and entry docs in carry-on.
  • Medicines packed for 35–40 days.
  • Weather re-checked for all three cities.
